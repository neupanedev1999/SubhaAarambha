About Us

Subha Aarambha Papers is a pioneering company dedicated to creating high-quality, eco-friendly, and biodegradable products from rice hay. We believe in sustainable development and are committed to reducing the environmental impact of our products.

Our mission is to offer an alternative to conventional paper products, using natural and renewable resources to create items that are not only functional but also environmentally responsible.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Process

At Subha Aarambha Papers, we take pride in our unique production process. By using rice hay, a byproduct of rice cultivation, we are able to produce high-quality paper that is both biodegradable and environmentally friendly. Our process involves:

  1. Harvesting: Collecting rice hay from local farms.
  2. Processing: Converting hay into pulp through a sustainable process.
  3. Production: Crafting a variety of paper products from the pulp.
  4. Quality Control: Ensuring each product meets our rigorous quality standards.
